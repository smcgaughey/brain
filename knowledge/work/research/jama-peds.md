## Project Summary

Large cohort study examining the impact of emergency department (ED) pediatric readiness and pediatric inpatient services on child mortality across 11 states over a decade (2012-2021).

**Sample:** 2,416,030 children treated in 759 EDs across 11 states (2012-2021)
