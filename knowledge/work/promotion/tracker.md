#### Callback RCT Details
**Full Title:** The Impact of a Physician Callback Program on Return Visit Rates Among Patients Discharged from the Emergency Department, a Pragmatic Randomized Controlled Trial
**Authors:** Marshall (lead), Elman (stats), Black, Ready, Andrews, Brannan, McGaughey (results advisor)
**Status:** Manuscript draft complete with all sections (Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, References)
**Key Findings:** 
- ITT: 6.5% vs 7.5% return rate (aOR 0.91, p=0.503 - non-significant)

**Timeline:** Multi-month project, Go-live January 26, 2026  
**Scope:** Complete overhaul of ED consultation paging infrastructure

**Project Goals:**
- Improve visibility into consult process and turnaround time to initial recommendations
- Provide consultants consistent information (reason for consult, MRN, callback number)
- Provide alternative mechanism for ongoing communication with consulting teams
- Leverage automation and Epic Foundation functionality
- Meet needs of both AHPL and HMC ED teams

**Key Deliverables:**
- Redesigned order appearance with simplified search (single entry per service vs. resident/fellow/attending grains)
- 3 new order questions with cascading, conditional logic
- Auto-paging functionality for select services (Admission/Consultation triggers)
- New Consult Tracking ED Track Board View
- Order-based tracking statuses (Ordered ‚Üí Paged ‚Üí Responded ‚Üí Connected ‚Üí Initial Recs Provided)

- Turnaround time monitoring with alerts (yellow at 5 min, red at 10 min for paging; 90 min target for initial recommendations)
- Standardized page format with consistent patient information
- OPA (Overriding Practice Advisory) to prevent duplicate consult orders
- New dashboards and reports to track consult events
