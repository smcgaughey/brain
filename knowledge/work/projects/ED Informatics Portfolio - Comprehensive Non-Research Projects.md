### PROJECT 2: ED Consult Process Transformation
**Timeline:** August 2021 - January 2026 (go-live January 26, 2026)
**Status:** Live and operational

**Executive Summary:**
Advocated for and led comprehensive overhaul of ED consultation workflow, directing design of automated paging system and real-time tracking infrastructure. Project tracked across multiple reports demonstrating sustained multi-year effort.

**Key Contributions:**
- Initial scoping began August 2021 (Dev-1)
- Advocated for comprehensive overhaul approach rather than incremental fixes
- Led workflow design through bi-monthly meetings over 6-month period with Denise Sandall
- Directed specifications for consult order structure, paging logic, and trackboard functionality
- Designed "Brief Clinical Question" field for automated pages
- Created tracking mechanism for documenting time of initial consultant recommendations
- Target metrics established: 45 minutes to see patient, 90 minutes to verbal recommendations

**Impact:**
- Automated Epic-to-SPOK paging system eliminating manual notification
- Real-time trackboard documentation of consultant response times
- By January 2026: multiple services achieved 100% compliance with 90-minute recommendation timeframe
- Published in institutional Clinician Action communication

**Collaborators:** Laura Chess (project proposer), Denise Sandall (Epic technical build), Beech Burns (C-suite liaison), Renee Edwards (CMO), Andy Lauer (Professional Board Chair)
